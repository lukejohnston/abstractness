Find out the abstractness of something!

xkcd says:

Wikipedia trivia: If you take any article, click on the first link in the article text not in parentheses or italics, and then repeat, you will eventually end up at "Philosophy".
Inspired by this, abstractness is defined as the number of clicks it take to get from a wikipedia article to Philosophy. The fewer steps, the more abstract it is!
